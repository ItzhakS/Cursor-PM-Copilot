## Purpose & Audience
- Monthly external update for customers and high-priority prospects highlighting product progress, fixes, and value moments.
- Reinforce company positioning (e.g., "Our product makes [customers] more [valuable outcome]") with concrete proof points tied to reliability and speed.
- Optional internal summary can be derived separately if needed (internal objectives remain internal-only).

## Inputs & Data Sources
- Latest product releases, fixes, and roadmap progress (dev sync notes, UAT outcomes).
- Customer success stories, testimonials, or metrics (CX team).
- Reliability/usage stats (analytics dashboards).
- Design support availability (design/marketing team for layout/formatting).
- Newsletter templates stored in designated folder.

## Narrative Structure
1. Hook: headline that connects to profitability, speed, or reliability.
2. Product updates: brief release notes tied to customer value.
3. Customer spotlight or metric highlight.
4. Upcoming initiatives / call-to-action.
5. Optional leadership note (brief) if additional context is required.

## Review & Approval Flow
- Draft outline and content in shared doc; flag sections needing input.
- Route for quick review: CPO, CEO (if external), CX (for accuracy), design/marketing team.
- Confirm compliance or legal review if required by CFO/Chief of Staff.

## Distribution Checklist
- Final copy approved and formatted.
- Distribution list validated (CRM, email platform, internal channels).
- Schedule send time; ensure follow-up tasks/logs created.
- Archive final newsletter and performance metrics in initiative folder.
